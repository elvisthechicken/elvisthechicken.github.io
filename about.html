A long time ago, in a small city,
not far away....

On a Saturday..... In June......

It was a warm summers day, a man was born under a rainbow spouting forth from a Unicorn's vagina. ALEX COOK - THE CHOSEN ONE. Despite being born with blonde curly hair and looking like a girl he was to become the Jesus of drunks, a bounder and a cad. A man destined for legendary awesomeness and saving the universe (not necessarily in that order).

His path of drunken righteousness was fraught. At 15, he was shot in the face (not like that!) and survived. Not through any kind of divine intervention mind, but by having balls so big, that they have their own gravitational field and thus changed the trajectory of the projectile to miss his brain.

With his unlikely band of heroes, THE BRAIN TRUST, Alex strides the path to beer nirvana. It is upon this path that he stumbled upon THE PENGUIN (some people call her Amy). In order to save the Universe, a union must be made between The Penguin and Alex.

All is not well. For the evil overlord RITZY TONY will not allow such a union to take place unless Alex undertakes the challenge known as THE STAG DO. Trials and tribulations of epic proportions await to test our saviour and his alcholic mettle. Will he survive? Will he sacrifice the Brain Trust like the bounder he is? Time will tell...
